First Aid Tips

Basic First Aid Guidelines

Here are some essential first aid tips that could help you during an emergency:

  • CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation): Learn the correct chest compression and breathing techniques.
  • Bleeding Control: Apply direct pressure to the wound and elevate the injured area if possible.
  • Burns: For minor burns, run the burn under cool water. For severe burns, cover with a clean cloth and seek immediate help.
  • Choking: Learn how to perform the Heimlich maneuver to assist someone who is choking.
  • Fractures and Sprains: Immobilize the injured area and apply ice to reduce swelling.

When to Seek Professional Medical Help

If the injury or medical situation is severe, it's essential to seek professional medical attention immediately. Call emergency services if needed.
