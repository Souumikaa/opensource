Campus Security Overview

Our campus security team is committed to ensuring a safe environment for all students, faculty, and staff. The security office is open 24/7 and provides various services to keep the campus secure.

Security Services Offered

  • 24/7 Surveillance and Monitoring
  • Incident Response and Investigation
  • Emergency Alert System
  • Escort Services for Students
  • Lost and Found
