Campus Mental Health Services

We offer a variety of mental health services to support your well-being:

  • Counseling Services: Individual and group therapy sessions.
  • Crisis Support: Immediate support for students in emotional distress.
  • Wellness Programs: Workshops and seminars on stress management, mindfulness, and more.
  • Online Resources: Access to mental health apps and online tools for self-care.
